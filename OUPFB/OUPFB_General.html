GENERAL INFORMATION

Have a look at the course offerings on the OUPFB website. The website has information about course costs, dates, locations, etc.

The Biology Department posts notices of each course on a bulletin board in the hallway between D105 and D106. The board also indicates which courses Trent has guaranteed spaces in. These notices also show the course outlines for each course.

Trent has guaranteed spots in some courses. If you register early you might be able to get one of these guaranteed spots.

Students often (but not always) get their first or second choice, regardless of whether we have a guaranteed space.

Registration occurs towards the end of January in each year. We will send around announcements by email to let you know about registration dates.

OUPFB courses are run through the Biology department. Biology students should direct their questions to marceldorken@trentu.ca or schow@trentu.ca. Students in the ERS program should contact dhoward@trentu.ca for information not covered on these pages.

Most courses are summer courses (must be paid for as summer courses). In many cases (particularly late summer courses) grades may not arrive in time to be submitted as summer courses and an incomplete will be recorded. This will be updated when the mark arrives. If you are planning to graduate, consider the time of your course.
